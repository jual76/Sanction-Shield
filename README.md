Risk Scoring Algorithm
AI-powered risk assessment assigns scores (1-100) based on:
- Sanctions list proximity (exact match, close match, negative)
- Jurisdiction risk level (Venezuela = High, Spain = Medium, UK = Low)
- Government connections (current official = Critical, former = High)
- Business sector (oil/gas, defense, banking = High risk)
- Payment source origin (Venezuelan state bank = Critical)
- Entity ownership structure (complex = Higher risk)
- Historical compliance issues
- Adverse media sentiment analysis

Risk Bands:
🟢 Low (0-30): Standard intake process
🟡 Medium (31-60): Enhanced due diligence required
🟠 High (61-85): Partner approval + OFAC specialist review
🔴 Critical (86-100): Automatic block + mandatory escalation
